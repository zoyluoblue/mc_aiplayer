mc_aiplayer. Open source Minecraft AI player for GitHub. Meet Bob, the fake player who read the job description and took it personally. You type one goal: mine three diamonds. The language model understands the request. Then deterministic task machines do the real work: gather wood, craft tools, smelt iron, staircase down safely, and keep going until the diamonds show up. If Bob gets hungry, stuck, attacked, or considers lava soup, the safety net says nope. Open the Bob panel, watch health, inventory, task state, and brain status. mc_aiplayer: LLM plans, tasks execute, Bob survives.
